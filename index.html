J&L GREEN FARM

J&L Green Farm is operated by a husband and wife team, Jordan and Laura Green. They raise forest-raised pork, grass-fed beef and lamb, pastured poultry, turkey, and pastured eggs. J&L Green Farm also sells other products, including spices and skincare products. Jordan and Laura manage their own farm as well as multiple other farms in the Shenandoah Valley, healing the land by employing sustainable farming practices.

Owner

Joe Cloud serves as T&E Meats General Manager. Prior to making a mid-life career change into sustainable agriculture and the meat business, Joe had a successful career in private consultancy as an urban planner and landscape architect. He is a graduate of Harvard University’s School of Design, and has a strong background in project management. These days, Joe is typically found on-site in Harrisonburg, managing the needs of T&E Meat’s customer base.
